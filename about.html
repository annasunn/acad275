Unconventional Units Calculator

The Unconventional Units Calculator is designed to be a webpage where people can go to discover and express ordinary things in unorthodox ways.

Key features:
  • Calculating length/width/distance, weight, volume, and speed.
  • Visual representation of units (chart or lines of visuals).
  • Hover feature describing units and sources.
  • Users can submit their unique units to be calculated through the website.
